Abstract


   The impact of the COVID-19 epidemic as a result, preventive measures against COVID-19 are social distancing and wearing masks. People travel less to reduce the chance of infection and due to the spread of covid-19 virus also resulted in the current economy in a deteriorating direction Most of the companies were affected by the decline in revenue, for example, used car dealers.
   Each time the customer purchases a car, customers must to come at showroom and inspect the car by themselves. May result in a chance to be at risk of contracting COVID-19 that is currently spreading heavily even if there are preventive measures and another way that customers will be able to inspect the car is Viewing photos of cars the customer wants to check. But the picture can only show the car in certain angles. The customer may not see the view of the car with defects.
   The researcher developed guidelines for developing an online showroom. Our techniques in 3D modeling of cars include Photogrammetry, Structure From Motion (SFM), Multiple View Stereo (MVS) techniques, etc. After getting a 3D model of a car, the next step is to create a web application. case the web application contains A web page provided for those who want to create 3D models, submit videos or pictures of the taken cars or motorcycles for developers to create. and there is a web page for showing 3D cars and motorcycles online.
   The benefit of this work is Develop guidelines for developing online showrooms for those who want to inspect the car can check the car online.
